vijay

Joseph Vijay,better known popularly as Vijay, is an Indian film actor and playback singer who works in Tamil cinema (Kollywood).
His films are often dubbed into Telugu, Hindi, Malayalam and Mandarin Chinese.
He is one of the highest paid actors in Tamil cinema.
Vijay is referred to by the media as Illaya Thalapathy and also as Thalapathy (commander).
Including Indian diaspora worldwide and China, he has significant following in Japan, UK and France.
